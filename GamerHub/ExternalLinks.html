External Links to Some Very Cool People in the VideoGame Commuity!

Below are links to youtube channels that are just as crazy about video games as we are! They hunt them, play them, and review them. Give them a look!.

Game Chasers:These guys go have fun doing the things we collecor's love... Hunting games! Check them out.

Pixel Game Squad:A group of buddies comparable to the Game Chasers but are a little more "Family Friendly"

Wood:Wood is a great guy to see latest reviews on games that are very honest and helpful for interested gamers!

Metal Jesus: Metal Jesus is one of the original collector's on Youtube having over 7,000 games! He focuses on buying guides and hidden gems so he is great for the starting collector.

Game Grumps:If you are not quite into game collecting and want to just relax and laugh while watching people play games, then the Game Grumps will satisfy your needs.
